# Global-Airbnb-Performance-Dashboard

## 1.Dashboard Summary 
Developed an interactive Airbnb Performance Dashboard to evaluate global market trends, customer preferences, and business performance. Identified expansion opportunities in high-demand cities, key drivers of booking behavior, regional service quality gaps, and low customer retention despite strong satisfaction scores, enabling data-driven business recommendations.

## 2.Project Objectives
-• Analyze Airbnb's global market presence to identify expansion opportunities in high-demand cities.
-• Evaluate accommodation preferences and booking patterns to understand key drivers of customer demand.
-• Assess guest satisfaction across locations to uncover service quality gaps and improvement areas.
-• Measure customer retention behavior and identify factors limiting repeat bookings despite positive guest experiences.
